How it works

The Operator
Loop

It takes in customer, purchase, engagement, and lifecycle signals. It scores risk and opportunity. It classifies the customer state. It activates the right recovery logic. And it tracks what happens next. That is the difference.

Step 01
Ingest
Pulls in customer data, purchase history, engagement signals, and lifecycle events from the connected Shopify store.
Step 02
Score
Oracle Intelligence Engine scores risk and opportunity — health score, urgency rating, and recovery potential for every customer.
Step 03
Classify
Each customer is assigned a precise state — Abandoned Cart, Dormant Buyer, Failed Payment, Repeat At-Risk, Replenishment Ready, or Engaged Unconverted.
Step 04
Recover
The right workflow triggers. AI-led recovery conversations begin. Personalised, state-specific, brand-intelligent outreach — executed autonomously.
Step 05
Track
Every action and outcome measured. Recovered revenue, workflow performance, and customer health trends reported in real time.
